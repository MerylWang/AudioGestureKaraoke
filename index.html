mUwUsic Karaoke

Welcome to mUwUsic: The Audio-Gesture Karaoke Machine!!!
In order to relay a voice command, begin your command with the keyword "karaoke."
  • Instructions - Voice:
    • 'play','resume' - Plays the Video
    • 'pause','stop' - Pauses the Video
    • 'restart' - Restarts the Video
    • 'louder' - Increases the volume
    • 'softer' - Decreases the volume
    • 'add [song name]' - adds song to queue
  • Instructions - Gesture:
    • Tap - Plays/Pauses the Video
    • Swipe Up - Increases the Volume
    • Swipe Down - Decreases the Volume
    • Swipe Right - Next Song
    • Circle - Restarts the Video
